Numerology $=$ Numerus (Numbers) $+$ Logos ( words, thoughts and expression)

Numerology is any belief in the divine or mystical relationship between a number and one or more coinciding events. It is also the study of the numerical value of the letters in words, names, and ideas. Numerology the English term came into existence in 1907 

Types of Numerology 

Kabbalah Numerology – Hebrew mysticism outgrowth of Hebrew alphabet with its 22 vibrations. Later it was adapted for the Greek alphabet and then Roman alphabet. The $1 3 ^ { \mathrm { t h } }$ century Kabbalists believed that the Old Testament ( Bible) was written in a secret code inspired by god. They used numerology as a tool to decipher this code. Twenty two base numerology also adds significant dimension to the interpretation of the twenty two Major Arcana of the Tarot deck. Also known as gematria or number symbols. 

Chaldean Numerology – closely related to Astrology originated in Mesopotamia, which is also considered as birth place of western astrology. Each letter has unique vibration and is assigned a number from 1 to 8 based on its energetic quality. The number 9 is not included as it is considered as sacred number. Single digits reveal the outer nature of the person, while double digits describe inner qualities.
