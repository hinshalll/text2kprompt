# INTRODUCTION

Life is the greatest mystery of all... and despite being its active participants, it's very likely that we will never truly be able to grasp its greatness or complexity. And while we can sometimes feel very small and insignificant when it comes to life overall, the fact is that we are still responsible for our own private universe - our own life - where our actions can make a difference. It's easy for us to forget this as our lives are in constant flux and change, which can often be quite overwhelming. It can also cause us to forget that each life is a precious gift - one that comes with an expiration date. Being busy living our lives, growing as we live, we have a limited amount of time to understand what our life is all about, to try and become the best version of ourselves possible. 

An increased awareness of our life path can help facilitate our growth. Amongst the numerous methods, the ancient technique of the Chinese Four Pillars stands out. It can also affect the way we think, feel and act. It is these patterns (and strong and weak Elements) that dictate our specific interests and desires, and also determine the way we typically do things. Whether we are quiet or loud, introverted or extroverted; whether we will act and follow our ambitions or simply rely on others, waiting patiently for things to come to us... this all depends on the Elements. They also define whether we will be strong willed or yielding; persistent or unsteady; ambitious or unassuming; driven by ideas or material items...

# About the Author

Althea is an author, consultant, and practitioner of Feng Shui and Four Pillars Astrology. Her formal studies began three decades ago, and carried her through south-east Asia studying with masters, and learning her craft. She worked tirelessly to unpack the ancient knowledge of these systems, which had for so many centuries been carefully concealed. All this time, she put her theory into practice in her own life. 

One reason the arts of Feng Shui and Four Pillars Astrology were so appealing to the author is their practical functionality in everyday life. These are not philosophical ideals (though in the highest forms they approach such). These are ways to understand and interact with the living world all around us. 

Currently, Althea lives in Europe; she writes daily, and consults both for individuals and corporations, and enjoys many long term clients. Althea is an experienced full time consultant and lecturer at Althea S.T. Consultants and the founder of Four Pillars, Feng Shui, Yi Jing & Xuan Kong Da Gua comprehensive study programs. Her professional approach across all fields remains open and giving, and her study program is carefully designed to give plenty of practical, useful and authentic information, and to equip her students with everything they might need to establish their own quality practice. 

After 3 decades of growth, practice, and experience, she offers the personalized approach to understanding the ways of Feng Shui and Four Pillars Astrology in her books: "A Course in Feng Shui: Make Your Property Work for You, at All Times" and "A Course in Chinese Astrology: Reveal Your Destiny, Harness Your Luck".
